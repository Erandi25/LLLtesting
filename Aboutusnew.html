About Us

Welcome to our Online Blood Bank System!

Who We Are

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between blood donors and those needing blood, providing a seamless and efficient platform for saving lives.

At Life Line Locator, we proudly introduce Sri Lanka’s first-ever online platform that unites blood and organ donors, recipients, and hospitals on a single, efficient network. This groundbreaking initiative addresses the critical challenges in managing lifesaving resources, ensuring that every donor’s gift reaches those who need it most, without unnecessary delays.

Our platform goes beyond traditional blood donation by incorporating organ donation services. We provide a secure and transparent environment for individuals to pledge their organs and for hospitals to manage organ transplants effectively. Whether it’s kidney, liver, or tissue donations, Life Line Locator facilitates ethical and timely matches, maximizing the potential to save lives.


Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the availability of safe and sufficient blood supplies to hospitals and patients. By connecting donors and receivers in real time, we strive to create a reliable network for life-saving blood donations.


Key Features

  • Communication: Real-time connection between donors, receivers, and hospitals to ensure rapid response during emergencies.
  • Comprehensive Donor Management: Enabling individuals to register as blood and organ donors with easy-to-use online tools.
  • Real-Time Availability: Instant updates on blood stock levels and available organs for hospitals and registered users.
  • Hospital Integration: Partnering with hospitals across Sri Lanka for transparent and efficient collaboration in lifesaving procedures.
  • User Friendly Interfaces
