Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal information provided to our website. By using our website, you agree to the practices outlined in this privacy policy.


2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register as a donor or recipient, we collect personal details such as your name, contact information, and location.

Health Information

If you are donating blood, we may collect health-related information to ensure the safety and eligibility of donations.

Usage Data

We collect data about how you use our website, including your IP address, browser type, and interaction with our services.


3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: The data is used to offer and maintain the services users sign up for.
  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, or marketing information (usually based on consent).
  • Legal Requirements: The organization might need to use personal data to comply with legal obligations, such as medical records or donation eligibility.
  • Improvement of Services: Usage data can help optimize the website and enhance the user experience by identifying trends or issues.
