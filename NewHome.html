Facts About Blood Needs

In Sri Lanka, blood is needed approximately every 4 seconds to support critical medical treatments.

Nearly 2,500 units of blood are required daily to meet the demand in hospitals across the country, particularly for surgeries, accidents, and illnesses like dengue and cancer.

Additionally, organ transplants such as kidneys and livers are in high demand, with hundreds of patients waiting for suitable donors to save their lives.
